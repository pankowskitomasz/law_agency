The Two Legal Systems

Private law applies to relationships between individuals in a legal system. e.g. contracts and labor laws. Public law applies to the relationship between an individual and the government. e.g. criminal law.

What Is It ?

The public court system is owned and operated by the government and is available to everyone, with or without a lawyer.

Why Use It ?
  • Offers free court resources, such as the time required for judges, court staff and facilities.
  • The legal process can be started anytime, by either party.
  • Great option when one of the parties is abusive or non-cooperative.
